# Client Bureau Keyword and Acquisition Strategy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

## Executive Direction

Client Bureau should win two markets at once:

1. **Demand capture**: high-intent searches from contractors and service businesses who already have a painful problem.
2. **Category creation**: searches around checking, rating, and documenting clients before accepting work.

The highest-demand language is not yet "client intelligence SaaS." Contractors search in plain pain terms:

- "customer won't pay contractor"
- "client won't pay contractor"
- "file mechanics lien Florida"
- "notice to owner Florida"
- "contractor unpaid invoice"
- "contractor contract template"
- "change order template contractor"

Client Bureau's category language should stay simple:

> Check the client before you take the job.

Secondary hook:

> One search could save thousands.

## Research Notes

Public autocomplete and SERP review show stronger existing demand around payment, lien, contract, and change-order problems than around the new "client rating for contractors" category. That means Client Bureau should use payment-protection keywords to acquire users, then introduce the broader platform once they land.

Exact search volume and CPC should be confirmed inside Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, Semrush, or live Google Ads test campaigns. Google describes Keyword Planner as a tool for finding new keywords, analyzing search trends, and estimating bids.

## Competitor/Market Gaps

### NoPayHomes

Positioned around screening properties/homeowners and reporting non-payment. Their language is punchy and aggressive. Client Bureau should compete with a more official, moderated, legally careful trust-platform voice.

Opportunity: "serious, professional, moderated, response-aware" instead of emotional or inflammatory.

### PayProof

Focused on contractor payment recovery packets: organizing invoices, photos, texts, approvals, and timelines.

Opportunity: Client Bureau can go wider by combining search, public client profiles, contract packets, evidence vault, recovery, and lien services.

### Zindo

Focused on attorney-backed invoice recovery for businesses.

Opportunity: Client Bureau can specialize in contractors and home/service jobs, where project documentation, change orders, lien deadlines, and client screening matter together.

### Notice-to-Owner Services

Florida notice providers compete on speed, deadlines, and construction-document service.

Opportunity: Client Bureau can package Notice to Owner, lien filing, payment recovery, and contractor documentation in one dashboard.

## Programmatic SEO Plan

### Public Client Profiles

Continue building indexable profiles only for admin-approved public client profiles.

Metadata pattern:

`[Name] [City, State] Client Bureau Profile | Contractor-Submitted Reports`

H1 pattern:

`[Name] in [City, State]: Client Bureau Public Profile`

Required language:

- contractor-submitted
- moderated
- reported experience
- client response
- evidence reviewed privately

Avoid:

- blacklist
- scammer
- deadbeat
- fraudster
- guilty
- fake star ratings in structured data

### Directories

Use approved public profiles only:

- `/clients`
- `/clients/florida`
- `/clients/florida/orlando`
- `/reports/recent`
- future `/industry/[trade]`
- future `/industry/[trade]/[state]`
- future `/industry/[trade]/[state]/[city]`

Do not publish empty keyword city pages. Each page should include real approved profiles, useful local context, and clear moderation/right-of-response language.

## Legal and Trust Guardrails

Use:

- reported experience
- contractor-submitted
- admin-reviewed
- moderated summary
- evidence reviewed privately
- client response available
- private matching
- payment-risk indicators

Avoid:

- blacklist
- expose
- shame
- fraud
- deadbeat
- scammer
- guilty
- guaranteed collection
- guaranteed lien validity
- automatic legal filing without review

Florida lien pages must clearly say:

- Florida first
- contractor authorization required
- attorney/vendor review before real filing
- no legal advice
- no guarantee of collection, priority, or enforceability
- contractor-direct payment only
